2、装饰者模式

装饰者模式（Decorate pattern）: 动态的给一个对象添加一些额外的职责，就增加功能来说，装饰模式比生成子类给为灵活

Decorate pattern:Decorator is a structural design pattern that lets you attach new behaviors to objects by placing these objects inside special wrapper objects that contain the behaviors.
